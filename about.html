Welcome to King of Glory Pentecostal Assembly

We're delighted to have you here. At King of Glory, we believe in the power of worship, prayer, fellowship, and the Word of God. Join us as we grow together in faith.

Mission Statement

Our Mission

To glorify God by making disciples of Jesus Christ through worship, teaching, fellowship, and outreach, empowering believers to live out their faith in daily life.

Our Vision

To be a vibrant, Christ-centered community that transforms lives and impacts the world through the love and power of Jesus Christ.

Our Core Values

• Faith in Jesus Christ as Lord and Savior

• Commitment to Biblical teaching and living

• Love and compassion for all people

• Unity and fellowship among believers

• Service and outreach to the community

Our Beliefs

We believe in the Trinity, the authority of the Bible, salvation through faith in Jesus Christ, the power of the Holy Spirit, and the importance of the local church in nurturing spiritual growth and community.
